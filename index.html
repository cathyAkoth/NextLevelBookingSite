What we are all about.

We are Esports

We are a Ugandan based Esports centered company with the main goal of professionalizing Esports in Uganda and extending in to other parts of Africa starting with our east African neighbors. Esports clearly has massive potential and very fertile ground in Africa.

We are optimistic of the future and look forward to having these games along with the teams televised live to attract more fans, partners, fanatics and enthusiasts. We will organise Esports events as way to source out and professionalize more talent among Esports Athletes to represent Uganda and Africa on the International Arena.

Among the events, we will have tournaments that venture in to the various domain of Esports with games such as FIFA, Formula 1, WRC, Fortnite, Call of Duty, Over Watch and League of Leagues among others. This vision is made possible by the current partners we have. Anyone can also become a partner.
